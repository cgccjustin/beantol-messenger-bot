1. Executive Summary

The Beantol Messenger AI Bot is a 24/7 AI sales and customer support assistant that lives inside your Facebook Page and Instagram inbox. It answers product questions, helps customers choose beans, generates quotes, books shop visits, captures leads and orders, and escalates to human staff when needed — all without making customers leave Messenger or Instagram.

This document explains what the bot does, how it works, where to change things, and what was built — useful for internal review or for showing other shop owners what a similar system could do for their business.

Platform: Facebook Messenger + Instagram DMs
Hosting: Render (beantol-bot.onrender.com)
AI: OpenAI gpt-4o-mini with RAG knowledge search
Data: Google Docs (knowledge) + Google Sheets (CRM)

4. How It Works

Customer sends message (Messenger or Instagram) │ ▼ Meta Webhook → Beantol Bot Server (Render cloud) │ ├── Check: Is admin already replying? → Pause bot ├── Check: Structured flows (quote, delivery, appointment, etc.) ├── Search Google Doc knowledge (RAG) for relevant facts ├── Load: behavior rules, inventory, chat history │ ▼ OpenAI generates a friendly reply │ ├── Send reply to customer ├── Save lead/order/quote/appointment if applicable └── Email team if handoff or delivery alert needed

Message processing order (simplified)

  1. Admin takeover — skip if staff is actively chatting
  2. Get Started / first-time welcome
  3. Reply-to context (swipe-reply YES on a specific bot message)
  4. Agent handoff acceptance
  5. Quote confirmation flow
  6. Post-quote follow-up
  7. Payment proof handling
  8. Outside-Cebu delivery rules
  9. Cebu area delivery (Maxim vs Naga vs province)
  10. Weekend pickup vs delivery rules
  11. Delivery rep YES acceptance
  12. Phrase-based human handoff
  13. Appointment booking wizard
  14. Product recommendation wizard
  15. AI reply (RAG + rules + inventory + history)
  16. Post-AI quote generation if pricing was discussed

Critical business rules run before the AI, so the bot stays consistent even when the AI would otherwise improvise.

5. Feature List

5.1 AI Customer Support

  • Answers hours, location, products, pricing, payment, brewing, storage, contacts
  • Replies in English (even if customer writes Cebuano/Tagalog/Bislish)
  • Conversation memory for context across messages
  • RAG search of Google Doc — does not guess prices or products

5.2 AI Sales Assistant

  • Consultative selling: brew method, taste, volume
  • Recommends 1–2 beans with explanations
  • Correct retail sizes and wholesale rules (6 kg MOQ for Prime, Santos, Cerrado only)
  • Respects out-of-stock inventory
  • Follow-up for stale quoted leads

5.3 Product Recommendations

Guided step-by-step picker: brew method → taste → 1–2 bean suggestions with prices.

5.4 Quotation System

  • Formal quotes with ID (QT-…) and printable shareable page
  • Saved to Google Sheets Quotes tab

5.5 Delivery Workflows

ZoneMethod
Cebu City, Mandaue, Talisay, Lapu-LapuMaxim (customer pays rider fee); payment + proof required first
Remote Cebu Province (Naga, Carcar, etc.)Pickup, own logistics, or J&T/courier
Outside Cebu / nationwideJ&T or preferred courier; team confirms cost/time

Delivery alerts email the team. Delivery does not pause the bot.

5.6 Human Handoff

  • Triggers on phrases like "talk to a human", "real person", "need agent"
  • Email alert + dashboard shows "Awaiting agent"
  • AI keeps answering until staff replies in Business Suite → bot pauses
  • Auto-resumes after 15 min admin idle, or manual Resume AI
  • Live agents: 9 AM–9 PM Philippine time daily

5.7 Appointment Booking

  • Shop visit booking via chat
  • Natural dates: "today 5pm", "tomorrow", "this coming Thursday"
  • Saved to Google Sheets Appointments tab

5.9 Live Inventory

Google Sheet Inventory tab (preferred) or UNAVAILABLE_PRODUCTS env. Toggle stock without redeploy.

5.10 Admin Dashboard

Tabs: Overview, Analytics, Sales, Leads, Appointments, Orders, Quotes, Inventory. Includes sync knowledge, test email, resume AI.

5.11 Other Features

  • Email notifications (Resend) for handoff and delivery
  • Reply-to message support (swipe YES maps to specific bot message)
  • Smart welcome (Get Started + first message handling)
  • Weekend/shop hours logic (Mon–Fri shop; agents daily 9 AM–9 PM)
  • Payment proof flow (GCash / UnionBank)

9. Knowledge Base (RAG)

  1. Google Doc indexed into searchable chunks (embeddings)
  2. Each message finds the 6 most relevant chunks
  3. Chunks + behavior rules sent to OpenAI
  4. AI writes a natural reply grounded in your content

Team edits Google Doc without code changes. Sync after edits.

10. Human Handoff Flow

Customer asks for human → handoff message + email → AI still answers follow-ups → Staff replies in Business Suite → bot PAUSES → After idle or Resume AI → "assistant is back" message

14. Pitch for Other Shops

Any product-based business with repeated DMs (retail, F&B, roastery, boutique) benefits from:

  • 24/7 professional first response
  • Consistent pricing and policy answers
  • Structured order/inquiry capture
  • Human takeover when it matters
  • Team-editable knowledge (Google Doc)
  • Admin dashboard without building a full app

To build one: Meta Developer app, OpenAI account, cloud host, Google Workspace (optional), custom development for products and rules.
