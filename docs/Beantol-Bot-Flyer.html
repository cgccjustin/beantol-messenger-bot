What it does for customers

  • Answers hours, prices, products & FAQ instantly
  • Recommends the right product (guided picker)
  • Generates formal quotes with shareable links
  • Books shop visits & captures delivery details
  • Explains payment (GCash, bank) & delivery options
  • Connects to a real person when they ask

What it does for your team

  • Email alerts when someone needs a human
  • Admin dashboard — leads, orders, quotes, inventory
  • Edit knowledge in Google Docs (no coder needed)
  • Toggle out-of-stock from a Google Sheet
  • Pause bot when you reply; resume with one tap
  • Works on Facebook Messenger & Instagram DMs

How it works (simple)

  1. Customer DMs your Page — same Messenger/Instagram they already use
  2. Bot searches your Google Doc for facts + applies your business rules
  3. OpenAI writes a friendly reply — grounded in your content, not guesses
  4. Important inquiries saved to Google Sheets; team emailed when needed
  5. You take over in Business Suite when the conversation needs a human touch

Real example: Beantol Coffee

A Cebu roastery serving 30+ cafés uses this bot to handle pricing, bean recommendations, Maxim delivery (Cebu metro), J&T shipping (province/nationwide), wholesale MOQ rules, and appointment booking — while staff focus on high-value chats.

Perfect for shops that…

  • Get the same questions every day in DMs
  • Sell products with sizes, variants, or delivery zones
  • Want leads captured, not lost in inbox scroll
  • Need after-hours coverage without extra staff

What you need to build one
